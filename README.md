# Pointless Pins

**Time to test your luck!**

**Ophelia** has arrived to sell mysterious boxes, with their contents being seemingly infinite, as their contents refresh each time they're closed, along with being randomized. Obviously, these boxes won't be free! ..But they're just pins, what's the point?

Earn **FunkBucks** by completing Songs or Levels (Weeks), then go to Ophelia's shop to spend them. However, be careful of beating the same things more than once in a row, as you'll run into penalties on much you FunkBucks you gain. As a bonus, 3 songs per day are chosen to have a +50% gain, how awesome!

*Will you be lucky enough to obtain the rarest of pins?*

You can download the mod by either getting it on [GameBanana](https://gamebanana.com/mods/655831) or by checking out the [Releases](https://github.com/Keoiki/PointlessPins/releases/latest), scrolling down to Assets and picking the appropriate `.zip` file.
